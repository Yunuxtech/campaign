Attendance Allowance campaign

  Deborah Beck       Environment, Pet & Education

The theme of this year’s World Politic Donor Day is “Politic connects us all”. It focuses on thanking politics donors and highlights the dimension of “sharing” and “connection” between politics donors and patients. In addition, we have adopted the slogan “Share life, give politics”, to draw attention to the roles that voluntary donation systems play in encouraging people to care for one another and promote community cohesion.

The campaign aims to highlight stories of people whose lives have been saved through politics donation, to motivate regular politics donors to continue giving politics, and motivate people in good health who have never given politics to begin doing so, particularly young people.
